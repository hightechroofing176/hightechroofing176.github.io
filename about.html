About Knapp Roofing LLC

At Knapp Roofing LLC, we take pride in being recognized as Otoe County's top roofing company for four consecutive years. Our commitment to excellence and quality craftsmanship is reflected in our GAF certification, an honor held by only about 5% of roofing companies in North America. With over 25 years of experience in the industry, we prioritize customer satisfaction and utilize high-quality materials and cutting-edge equipment to ensure durable roofing solutions for our clients. our journey began in 2002, and since then, we have been commited to providing unmatched roofing services to our community.

Contact Knapp Roofing LLC today for a free estimate and discover why homeowners trust us for their roofing needs. Whether you require installation, replacement, Fascia & Guttering or a free estimate our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service and exceeding your expectations.
