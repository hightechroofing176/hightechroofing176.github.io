ABOUT US

About Stag Roofing: Your Local Roofing Experts

At Stag Roofing, we bring over 15 years of hands-on roofing experience to homes across Devon and Somerset. Fully qualified with an NVQ Level 2 in Pitched Roofing, we specialise in everything from pitched and flat roofs to full replacements and reliable roof repairs. We also offer professional loft conversions — perfect for transforming storage into valuable roof or living space.

We’re proud of our 5-star ratings on Bark and MyBuilder, which reflect our reliable services, transparent pricing, and consistently high standards of workmanship. Every member of our team is committed to doing the job right the first time, with clear communication and respect for your home. All our roofing work is backed by a guarantee, giving you complete peace of mind.
