Slating & Tiling

At Stag Roofing, we provide expert slating and tiling services for homes across Devon and Somerset. With over 15 years of hands-on roofing experience, our team is fully qualified and equipped to handle everything from small tile repairs to full roof replacements on pitched roofs. We work with care and precision to deliver clean, durable results that protect your home for the long term.

The Importance of Quality Slating and Tiling

Your roof is your home’s first defence against the weather, and damaged or poorly installed tiles can lead to serious issues like water ingress, damp, and heat loss. Professionally installed slates and tiles don’t just protect your property — they improve its appearance and add value. We use high-quality materials and take the time to get it right.

Our Approach

Every slating and tiling job we take on is treated with the same attention to detail, whether it’s a simple repair or a full re-roof. We’ll assess the condition of your roof, explain your options clearly, and carry out the work using reliable, proven techniques. We always aim to keep disruption to a minimum and leave your home tidy and secure once the job is done.

Why Choose Stag Roofing?

With over 15 years of roofing experience and a 5-star reputation on Bark and MyBuilder, Stag Roofing is trusted by homeowners across the South West. We’re fully qualified, offer guarantees on our work, and provide honest advice with no pressure.
